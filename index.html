Frequently Asked Questions

How long does printing take?

The turnaround time depends entirely on the service and quantity. **Standard business card** orders usually take **3-5 business days** after design approval. For items like **wedding cards or large-volume flyers**, it may take longer. Please discuss your deadline with our team when you submit your inquiry for an accurate estimate.

Can I get custom designs?

Absolutely! **Custom design** is our specialty. Our services include dedicated **Graphic Design** and **Logo Design**. Whether you need a brand-new logo or a unique layout for a wedding invitation, our team will work with you to create a design that perfectly matches your vision and requirements.

Do you deliver outside Hyderabad?

While our main office is in Chatta Bazar, **Hyderabad**, we are happy to arrange delivery for our printing services to locations **outside of Hyderabad**. Shipping charges will apply based on the delivery location and the size/weight of the order. Please specify your delivery address in your inquiry for an accurate shipping quote.
