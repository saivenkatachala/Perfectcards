Frequently Asked Questions

How long does printing take?

The completion time depends on the type of service and the quantity of your order.Standard business card orders are typically ready within 3–5 business days after design approval.Projects such as wedding cards or large-volume flyers may require additional production time.To ensure timely delivery, please share your preferred deadline with our team when submitting your inquiry — we’ll provide an accurate timeline based on your requirements.

Can I get custom designs?

We take pride in offering custom design solutions tailored to your unique needs.Our dedicated team specializes in Graphic Design and Logo Design, ensuring every project reflects your vision with creativity and precision.Whether you’re looking for a brand-new logo or a custom layout for wedding invitations and other print materials, we’ll work closely with you to create designs that perfectly align with your style and requirements.

Do you deliver outside Hyderabad?

We proudly offer Pan-India and international delivery for all our printing services.Our main office is located in Chatta Bazar, Hyderabad, but we’re happy to arrange deliveries to locations outside Hyderabad as well.Shipping charges will vary based on the delivery destination and the size or weight of your order.Please include your delivery address in your inquiry so we can provide an accurate shipping quote.

Can I get both premium and budget-friendly options?

Yes! We offer multiple material and finish choices to suit every budget — from affordable paper prints to premium textured or laminated finishes.

Do you offer express or same-day printing?

Yes, we offer express printing for selected products within certain locations. Contact us directly to confirm availability.

What if I receive damaged or misprinted items?

We maintain strict quality control, but if there’s any issue with your order, we’ll reprint or replace it at no extra cost after verification.
